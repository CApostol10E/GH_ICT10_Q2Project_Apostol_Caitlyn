About the Club:

This club is about anime. What these members will learn is about how respected these animes are. And why it's important for us to learn about anime. We get to dive into anime. Some Anime are TV shows too, get ready to dive in!

Club Goals:

  • Learn about Anime
  • How Anime is Important

Activities:

  • Drawing anime characters
  • Writing your own Anime
  • Discussion about Anime

Membership Requirements:

  • Must know the basics of anime
  • Notebook for sketching, and notes
  • Bring coloring pencils, a pen, a pencil with the eraser, and sharperner
